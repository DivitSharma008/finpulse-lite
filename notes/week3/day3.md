# Day 3 — Sharpe Ratio

- Implemented the Sharpe Ratio using annualised mean return and annualised standard deviation.
- Used India's standard risk-free rate of **6.5%** as the benchmark.
- The strategy yielded ~3% annual return, which is below the risk-free rate, resulting in a **negative Sharpe Ratio** — correctly indicating the strategy underperformed relative to risk taken.
