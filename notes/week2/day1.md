# Day 1 — SMA Crossover Strategy

- Studied the SMA Crossover strategy to determine buy, sell, and hold signals for a given stock.
- The strategy compares the 50-day moving average against the 200-day moving average:
  - 50-day MA crosses above 200-day MA → **BUY**
  - 50-day MA crosses below 200-day MA → **SELL**
  - Otherwise → **HOLD**
